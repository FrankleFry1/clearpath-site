ClearPath PA

Prior authorization for biologics, handled in four steps.

ClearPath PA pulls the clinical chart from ModMed, uses AI to extract the relevant criteria, runs a pre-flight checklist against payer requirements, and tells your team whether a PA will be approved before anyone submits anything. Catch documentation gaps before the payer does.

1
Chart Pull
Demographics, diagnoses, medications, labs, and clinical notes retrieved directly from ModMed in one call.
2
AI Extraction
Relevant clinical history, prior treatment failures, and lab values extracted and mapped to payer criteria fields.
3
Pre-Flight Check
15-point checklist validates completeness before submission — missing data surfaces before the payer sees it.
4
PA Ready
Pre-Flight complete. Documentation gaps surfaced and resolved. Hand off to your existing submission workflow with confidence.
