What We Build

Two tools. One integration. Zero compromises.

Each ClearPath tool was designed around a specific pain point observed firsthand — not retrofitted from a generic platform.

Mohs Surgery

Day-sheet management with 38 anatomical diagram types, automated CPT documentation, charge capture to ModMed, and surgical packet PDF generation — replacing the whiteboard and Excel.

Prior Auth Intelligence

AI-assisted prior authorization for biologic medications. ClearPath PA pulls chart data directly from ModMed, extracts the clinical criteria that matter, and tells your team whether the PA will be approved — before it leaves the practice.

ModMed Native

Both tools connect directly to ModMed via the synapSYS API — patient lookup, encounter data, charge posting, and document attachment all happen without leaving the workflow.
